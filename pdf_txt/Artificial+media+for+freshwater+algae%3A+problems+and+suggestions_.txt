AF-6 Medium  
Freshwater medium adapted for Botryococcus 
Reference : Provasoli, L. and Pinter, I. J. (1959). Artificial media for fresh -water algae: problems and suggestions. In: Tryon, C. A., Jr. and 
Hartmann, R. T. (eds.) The Ecology of Algae. Spec. Pub. No. 2, Pymatuning Laboratory of Field Biology, Pittsburgh pp 84- 96.

g L-1 DEIONISED WATER ( dH2O) QUANTITY FOR MEDIUM

To p repare AF-6 Medium  (1 L)  
• Add MES ( 1) to 990.0 mL dH 2O 
• Add each stock solution ( 2 – 13) in the stated volume to the MES solution .  
• Adjust the  pH to 6.5 – 7 with 20% NaOH . 
• Autoclave at 121°C (15 psi  for 20 mins) .
