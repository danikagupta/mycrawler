Research  Interests
 
1. Synthesis and Characterization of  Alloys, compounds and Minerals  
2. Preparation and characterization of nano - particles of spinel ferrites, ferro -fluids, dilute semiconductors and 
Perovs kites comprising these particles  
3. Crystal Structure determination of alloys and compounds using X -Ray powder  and Neutron  diffraction and 
Rietveld refinement technique.  
4. Investigation magnetic structures of bulk and nanomaterials using Neutron Powder Diffract ion. 
5. Systematic examination of low temperature evolution of magnetism in nanomaterials using SQUID 
magnetometry and Mössbauer  spectrosocpy.  
6. Study of disordered magnetism in nanoparticles using ac -susceptibility analysis and probing paradigmatic 
signatures of spin glasses/cluster spin glasses/ superspin glasses/ surface spin glasses using relaxation, 
ageing, and memory and rejuvenation effect.  
7. Study of defect induced magnetism by introducing vacancies via cationic substitutions in dilute 
semiconductors.  
8. Study of  optical, electrical and magnetic behaviors  of dilute Semiconductors  
9.
